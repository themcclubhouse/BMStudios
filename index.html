About Me

Hello, my name is MarieClaire Ololade Fadonougbo, but I also go by Bonnie M when I'm creating. I'm a proud African American artist who finds joy and freedom in exploring the endless possibilities of design.

As an introverted yet ambitious soul, I often take on more than I can handle—challenging myself to grow and evolve in different ways. My artistic journey is fueled by curiosity and my imagination, and I never limit myself to a single style or medium. I love getting lost in the creative process, experimenting with techniques, and letting my imagination guide me.

This portfolio is a glimpse into my world—a space where I share my passion for art, design, and creating in general. Whether you're here to browse, collaborate, or simply connect, I hope my work speaks to you. Welcome! :D
